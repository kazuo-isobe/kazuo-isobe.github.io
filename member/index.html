Kazuo Isobe / 磯部一夫, PI
He completed his Ph.D. in soil microbial ecology at The University of Tokyo. After working as an Assistant Professor at The University of Tokyo and a researcher at UC Irvine, in 2022, he joined Peking University as an Associate Professor.

Wen Guo - 郭雯, Postdoc
Wen joined our group in 2023 as a postdoctoral researcher. She earned her doctorate from the Chinese Academy of Forestry & International Center for Bamboo and Rattan and also studied at the Swiss Federal Institute for Forest, Snow and Landscape Research (WSL). She has a strong interest in deciphering plant-soil-microbe interactions.
Yaping Liu - 刘亚平, PhD student
Yaping joined our lab as a research assistant in 2022 and became a PhD student in 2023. During her master’s degree, she focused on the microbial biogeography. She is studying microbial interactions in soil formation for her PhD study.
Xiangxia Yang - 杨想霞, PhD student
Xiangxia joined our group as a PhD student in July 2023 after receiving her Master’s degree from Sun Yat-sen University. She is interested in soil organic matter formation and its interaction with soil microbes.
Mingming Xia - 夏明明, PhD student
Mingming joined our group in 2024 as a PhD student after receiving her Master’s degree from the Institute of Soil Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Nanjing. Her interests include the phylogeography of soil microbes in the context of global change.
Yaru Wei - 魏亚茹, Master student
Yaru joined our group in 2023 as a visiting Master’s student from Inner Mongolia University. Her research focuses on desert plants’ nitrogen acquisition.
Le Chang - 常乐, Research assistant

Xiaoding Lin - 林小丁, Research assistant
